cookie manager : manages all cookies ie adding or deleting a cookie.

    cookieMananger = new cookieManager();
    cookieHandler.Setdefault(cookieMananger);

CookieStore = stores and retrieves cookies.
cookieStore cookieStore = cookieManager.getCookieStore();

Cookie Policy:
1. CookiePolicy.ACCEPT_ORIGINAL_SERVER
2. CookiePolicy.ACCEPT_ALL
3. CookiePolicy.REJECT_ALL
